The Details

Sleep in a quiet, temperature-controlled space at 126 S. Second Street in Palatka, Florida. We accept adults and minors with a guardian from 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm. Then the doors are locked at 9:00 pm to provide a place to rest, free from disruption.

We provide a safe and welcoming place to stay overnight, offering security and peace of mind. Our doors are open from 7:00 PM to 7:00 AM, and no one in need will be turned away.

A warm, nourishing meal is available through Bread of Life. A nearby resource ready to serve you with care and dignity.

Access clean and private shower facilities to refresh and recharge. We provide essential toiletries so you can feel comfortable and cared for.

Your health and well-being matter. We can help connect you to local providers for medical care, counseling, and mental health support. Whether you need immediate assistance or ongoing care, we’re here to guide you.

We connect you with local resources, job training, and employment opportunities to help you take the next step toward independence.
